Our Story

Founded in 2015, Delicious Bistro began as a small kitchen tucked away in the heart of the city, driven by a love for fresh, locally‑sourced ingredients and a desire to create memorable dining experiences. Our founders, Emma and Luca, shared a vision of a place where seasonal produce, sustainable practices, and culinary creativity come together. Over the years, we have grown from a modest bistro to a beloved neighborhood destination, while staying true to our roots: farm‑to‑table philosophy, warm hospitality, and a menu that celebrates the flavors of each season.
